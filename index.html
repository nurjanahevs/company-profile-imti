Welcome to PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA

PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA is a national private contractor business entity which was established on November 17, 2020 in Bekasi. This business entity is engaged in the service business which includes Engineering Services, Civil Construction Services, Mechanical, Electrical and Suppliers.

The establishment of PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA is intended to take part in supporting Indonesia's National development program. with the support of professional human resources that we have, with the experience of a qualified founder in the world of construction projects, we are sure to meet client demands, especially for quality construction results, cost efficient, on schedule in time and givingsatisfaction to all Parties

    VISION & MISSION OF THE COMPANY

    To become a leading, modern, innovative and creative private contractor, so as to be able to assist all parties in the success of national development.

    • Creating a Transparent Construction Management System
    • Creating Reliable and Professional Human Resources
    • Creating client satisfaction
    • Always maintain and improve the quality of the work
