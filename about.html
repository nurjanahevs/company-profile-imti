About Our Company

PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA is a national private contractor business entity which was established on November 17, 2020 in Bekasi. This business entity is engaged in the service business which includes Engineering Services, Civil Construction Services, Mechanical, Electrical and Suppliers.

The establishment of PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA is intended to take part in supporting Indonesia's National development program. with the support of professional human resources that we have, with the experience of a qualified founder in the world of construction projects, we are sure to meet client demands, especially for quality construction results, cost efficient, on schedule in time and givingsatisfaction to all Parties.The main goals that we must achieve in carrying out our work for clients are:

  • Creating a work environment that is safe, comfortable, professional, clean and healthy, and Zero Work Accidents
  • Creating work with good quality, according to ISO or SNI standards or other standards that have received recognition from the construction world.
  • Offering competitive costs according to market prices
  • Carry out the work according to the agreed time.

Thus high hopes that will be etched by the Directors of PT. ARUNG MESTER TEHNIK INDONESIA is able to realize and help all clients in the present and in the future. Best regards,
